Introduction

At "NestBook", we are fully committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy explains how we collect personal information, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

When you register on the app or make a payment, we may ask you to provide some basic information to facilitate the service, including:

  • Full name.
  • Mobile phone number.
  • Email address (if any).
  • Details of the residential unit you are interested in.

2. How We Use Your Data

Our use of the information we collect is strictly limited to the essential purposes of operating the application, which are:

  • Providing the core service: Sending a notification to the property owner containing only your (name and phone number) to inform them of your interest in their unit and facilitate communication between you.
  • Payment management: Processing the application commission, confirming successful payments, and resolving any technical issues you may encounter during payment.
  • Technical support: Communicating with you to answer inquiries or resolve any problems you face in the app.

3. No Data Sharing with Third-Parties

At "NestBook", we deeply respect your privacy. We assure you that we do not sell, rent, share, or trade your personal data with any third parties (such as advertising companies or marketing agencies) under any circumstances.

4. Electronic Payment Security (Payment Gateway)

  • All electronic payment transactions (whether via bank cards or electronic wallets) are processed through a secure and certified payment gateway (Paymob).
  • The "NestBook" app does not collect, store, or retain any credit card numbers or secret PINs for electronic wallets on its own servers. This data is encrypted and processed directly by the payment service provider.

5. Data Security

We take all necessary technical and security measures to protect your registered data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Privacy Policy Amendments

We may update the privacy policy from time to time. You will be notified of any material changes to this policy via app notifications.
