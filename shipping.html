Last updated: April 2026

1. Delivery Method

Please note that the "NestBook" platform and website provide entirely digital and informational services. We do not sell or ship any physical goods that require delivery via traditional shipping companies.

2. Delivery Time

  • The service is executed and delivered instantly (Instant Delivery).
  • Once the electronic payment process is successfully completed (whether via bank cards or electronic wallets), the system automatically and without any human intervention displays the contact details of the owner of the selected residential unit directly on the user's screen.

3. Order Confirmation

After payment, a notification will appear to the user on the website confirming the success of the transaction, in addition to the appearance of the required information (owner's phone number/contact method) at the same moment, which constitutes a full completion of the "delivery" process on our part.
